Privacy Statement

Purposes and legal basis of the processing operation

Purposes of the processing operation

“EPstairs Web app” is a mobile application which displays the number of steps that a user takes. To count his or her activity (number of steps), the user has to scan QR codes when he or she is entering and exiting the stairways. Those QR codes are posted near some stairway entrances in the EU buildings.

Each user can see his or her record list of activities related to a given period. Rankings of the ten best climbers, calculated on a weekly basis and also your overall steps, are shared with all users of the application. In order to encourage participants, prizes could be awarded for the best climbers based on their rankings per category. Different categories could be used such as the status and gender of users.

Legal basis for processing personal data

The application is compliant with the Regulation (EC) No 45/2001 for the processing of personal data carried out by the European Parliament. The legal basis for the processing operations is the Article 5(a), Article 5(d) and Recital 27 of the Regulation (EC) No 45/2001.

Personal data used

The personal data described below are strictly necessary for the processing operation described in this Privacy Statement:

  • Email (with a “europa.eu” domain name)
  • Credentials (nickname and access token)
  • QR Code reference
  • Date (day)
  • Number of steps
  • History of activities (number of steps per day)
  • Cookies and similar technologies
  • Image (profile image, scanned image)
  • Data used for the award of prizes (such as the status, the gender of the user)

Use of personal data and who can view it

Some personal data are processed locally on the data subjects’ devices, corresponding to the client-side application, such as the profile and scanned images. On the server-side, the server only uses the personal data necessary for the processing operation described in this Privacy Statement.

Each user can see 4 rankings:

  • the top 10 of the week,
  • the overall top 10,
  • his or her current position of the week compared with those of the 2 users above and the 2 users below him or her in the ranking,
  • his or her current position of the overall ranking compared with those of the 2 users above and the 2 users below him or her in the ranking.

Each ranking displays the number of steps and the position of those users identified by their nickname. Only persons having an email with the “europa.eu” domain name can subscribe to this application, and can see these rankings.
