Dear colleagues,

Health benefits of stair climbing

Reduces

  • Blood pressure
  • Cholesterol
  • Stress and tensions
  • Risk of heart disease

Increases

  • Life expectancy
  • Bone density
  • Cardio vascular fitness

Helps you to

  • Boost your energy
  • Sleep better
  • Strengthen your muscles
  • Lose weight
  • Improve mood

Prevents

  • Cancer
  • Obesity
  • Osteoporosis
  • Other chronic diseases (diabetes)
